The people behind the capital.

Narp Capital is led by professionals with experience across real estate investment, development, and private equity. A small team with a shared conviction: build for the long term, and the returns take care of themselves.

Michael Cornish

Founder & Managing Partner

Michael founded Narp Capital with a clear mandate: build a real assets investment and development firm that takes a genuinely long-term view. Not a firm that talks about long-term investing—one that actually structures its capital, partnerships, and operations around it.

He leads investment strategy, acquisitions, and firm-wide partnerships, overseeing each project from opportunity identification through execution. Before founding Narp Capital, Michael spent over a decade working across real estate development, capital markets, and private investment.

Austin West

Director, Investments

Austin leads investment analysis, acquisition underwriting, and portfolio management across the firm's development pipeline. His focus is on identifying opportunities supported by genuine market fundamentals—not trend-driven narratives.

He works closely with the firm's leadership on new investments, financial structuring, and the strategic growth of Narp Capital's portfolio across both real estate and private equity.

Matthew McPhie

Director, Advisory

Matthew leads Narp Capital's advisory practice, working with landowners, investors, and development partners to evaluate opportunities, navigate complex transactions, and deliver real estate advice grounded in rigorous analysis.

His experience spans acquisitions, market analysis, and development strategy—enabling clients to make well-informed decisions at every stage of the investment process.

Every project begins with a disciplined investment decision.

At Narp Capital, we believe thoughtful leadership, patient capital, and exceptional execution create places that endure—contributing not only to the built environment, but to the long-term strength of the communities we serve.
